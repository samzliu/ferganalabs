What we do

Fergana Labs is building memory for consumer AI. Our mission is to enhance human agency by making your personal data work for, rather than against, you. Guided by a commitment to thoughtful design and technical breakthroughs, we’re creating experiences where technology fades into the background and people come first.

Who we are

We are a small team of builders and researchers based in San Francisco, CA.
